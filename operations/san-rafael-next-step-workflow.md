# San Rafael next-step workflow

## Purpose
This document turns the San Rafael / Colombia pilot into an operational sequence for fundraising launch and field follow-up.

## Current state
- San Rafael should now be treated as an active pilot, not a concept.
- First planting has already happened.
- Media evidence exists and should be treated as proof-of-action.
- The next priority is to launch a Zeffy campaign and convert the pilot into a controlled field expansion path.

## Immediate priorities
1. Launch the Zeffy campaign.
2. Build the evidence pack from field media and WhatsApp history.
3. Send a concise next-step brief to Djibril.
4. Align public wording across Zeffy and website content.

## Zeffy campaign logic
- Keep a single narrative: landslide and erosion mitigation through vetiver.
- Lead with one real field story and one visible intervention.
- Use the current pilot as proof that action has already started.
- Make the donation ask expansion-oriented, not hypothetical.
- Tie suggested donation amounts to concrete outputs where possible.

## Field follow-up logic
- Confirm what has already been planted.
- Confirm the immediate maintenance and survival follow-up.
- Clarify what conditions must be met before expanding beyond the pilot.
- Keep a clean distinction between confirmed facts and still-open questions.

## Minimum checklist
- [ ] Zeffy campaign draft finalized
- [ ] Hero image selected
- [ ] Planting proof selected
- [ ] Missing facts list written
- [ ] Djibril next-step brief sent
- [ ] Website wording aligned with campaign language

## Open facts to close
- Exact number of slips purchased
- Exact number planted
- Exact number remaining
- Exact planting date
- Site references and local actors
- Maintenance plan
- Conditions for broader scaling
