Field Update · April 2026

Vetiver hedgerows along the Kivu shoreline.

In March, VSF and local partners completed a 1.2 km vetiver hedgerow installation along an eroding riverbank in South Kivu. Early observations show reduced soil displacement after seasonal rains.

Vetiver Highway site, South Kivu, DRC — March 2026.

The installation follows VSF's field protocol adapted for high-humidity riparian zones. Local partners managed planting, spacing, and early monitoring. Vetiver slips were propagated on-site to reduce cost and build local familiarity with the material before broader application.

A follow-up site visit is planned for June. We will share comparative photos and an updated field note once the wet season data is collected.
