## How to plant

Planting vetiver is simple when the site is prepared carefully and the slips are healthy.

### 1. Prepare the site

- Clear weeds and mark the contour line.
- Check drainage and slope stability.
- Plan spacing before planting starts.

### 2. Prepare planting material

- Use fresh, healthy slips from a nursery.
- Keep roots moist before planting.
- Avoid weak or damaged material.

### 3. Plant the hedgerow

- Set slips along the contour line.
- Keep alignment consistent.
- Water immediately after planting.

### 4. Maintain the stand

- Water during establishment.
- Replace failed slips early.
- Monitor spacing, coverage, and runoff control.

The goal is not only to plant vetiver once. It is to establish a living system that can keep working with modest maintenance.
