# How to plant Vetiver System

This legacy guide is kept as an archive reference. The live site now uses a shorter public vetiver explanation and active project pages to show the work in context.

## Practical sequence

1. Confirm the site conditions and the erosion or runoff problem.
2. Prepare healthy planting material from a reliable nursery.
3. Plant along contour lines or the layout the site calls for.
4. Water, weed, and monitor early establishment.
5. Review results and adjust the layout if needed.
