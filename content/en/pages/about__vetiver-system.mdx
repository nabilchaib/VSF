## What it is

The Vetiver System uses vetiver grass as living infrastructure. Its deep roots slow runoff, hold soil in place, and make fragile land easier to manage.

## What it does

- Stabilizes slopes and embankments
- Reduces erosion and surface runoff
- Supports rehabilitation of degraded land
- Works with simple maintenance once established

## Why VSF uses it

VSF uses vetiver because it is practical, scalable, and easy to explain to communities, partners, and donors. It is not a theory page. It is the technical basis for the field work we support.
