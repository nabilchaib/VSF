<HtmlContent html={`<h1>About VSF</h1>
<h2>Why vetiver matters</h2>
<p>Vetiver is a practical living tool for soil, water, slopes, and fragile ground. It is most useful when people can apply it locally and keep using it over time.</p>
<h2>Why VSF exists</h2>
<p>Vetiver Sans Frontieres exists to help communities and partners turn vetiver knowledge into field-ready action through plain-language education, site-specific guidance, project support, and public accountability.</p>
<h2>What VSF does</h2>
<ul>
  <li>Explains vetiver in plain language.</li>
  <li>Assesses site conditions before recommending action.</li>
  <li>Supports projects and partnerships through implementation.</li>
  <li>Keeps project pages and updates visible to the public.</li>
</ul>
<h2>Why trust VSF</h2>
<p>VSF focuses on field logic, local ownership, and transparent follow-through so donors and partners can see what their support covers.</p>
