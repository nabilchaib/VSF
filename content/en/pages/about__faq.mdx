# FAQ

## About VSF

**What is VSF?**  
VSF is a nonprofit organization that helps communities use vetiver for soil protection, water management, and land rehabilitation.

**How does VSF create impact?**  
We focus on practical field support, public evidence, and local partnerships so the work can be understood, trusted, and maintained.

## Support

**How can I give?**  
Use the donation path on the site, or [contact VSF](/about/contact) if you want to support a specific project.

**How are donations used?**  
They support field work, project follow-through, evidence gathering, and the public assets that help donors understand the work.

## Accountability

**How do I know the work is real?**  
The public site is structured around projects, evidence, and clear next steps. The RDC hub is the best place to start if you want the flagship proof layer.

[Open the RDC hub](/projects/rdc) or [browse the projects gateway](/projects) to see how the pieces fit together.
