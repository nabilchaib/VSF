			<h2>Benefits of Sétiver Grass:
</h2>		
		<p>Sétiver grass offers a range of environmental and economic benefits, including:</p><ul><li><strong>Soil and Water Conservation</strong>: Reduces erosion by up to 90% and enhances water retention.</li><li><strong>Slope Stabilization</strong>: Prevents landslides, protecting infrastructure on steep slopes.</li><li><strong>Disaster Mitigation</strong>: Provides resilience against floods and heavy rainfall.</li><li><strong>Phytoremediation</strong>: Absorbs pollutants like heavy metals, improving soil and water quality.</li><li><strong>Agricultural Improvement</strong>: Boosts crop yields, reduces runoff, and acts as a natural pest barrier.</li><li><strong>Essential Oil Production</strong>: Sétiver oil is valued for its earthy scent and is used in perfumes, skincare, and wellness products.</li><li><strong>Skincare and Cosmetics</strong>: Rich in antioxidants, Sétiver oil helps with anti-aging, moisturizing, and treating acne.</li><li><strong>Aromatherapy</strong>: Promotes relaxation, reduces anxiety, and improves sleep quality.</li><li><strong>Holistic Health</strong>: Supports detoxification, circulation, and emotional well-being.</li><li><strong>Sustainable Handicrafts</strong>: Used in eco-friendly crafts, roofing, and animal fodder, supporting local economies.</li></ul>
